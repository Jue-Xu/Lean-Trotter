\begin{abstract}
We present the first machine-checked proof of the Lie--Trotter product
formula in \Lean with \Mathlib, together with higher-order extensions
(symmetric Strang, multi-operator, fourth-order Suzuki $S_4$) and
commutator-scaling error bounds obtained via a new Duhamel/FTC
technique that sidesteps ODE uniqueness theory. For the $S_4$
integrator, CAS-assisted Baker--Campbell--Hausdorff expansion yields
four order-$t^5$ bounds, including a provably strictly tighter
refinement of Childs et al.'s (2021) heuristic prefactors.
The formalization spans over 11\,400 lines with zero \texttt{sorry}
axioms and zero own theorem-level BCH-interface axioms; two
private axioms remain inside the companion
\href{https://github.com/Jue-Xu/Lean-BCH}{Lean-BCH} project (the
three-factor symmetric BCH quintic Taylor bridge and the new
five-factor palindromic septic identification).
\end{abstract}
